# Tools, datasets & courses

## Free, high-quality courses

- **[Neuromatch Academy — Computational Neuroscience](https://compneuro.neuromatch.io/)** — 3-week intensive, Python-first, taught by leading researchers. Run yourself or take it live in summer.
- **[Neuromatch Academy — Deep Learning](https://deeplearning.neuromatch.io/)** — sibling course, neuro-flavored DL.
- **[Neuromatch Academy — NeuroAI](https://neuroai.neuromatch.io/)** — newer course explicitly bridging the two.
- **[MIT 9.40 — Introduction to Neural Computation (OCW)](https://ocw.mit.edu/courses/9-40-introduction-to-neural-computation-spring-2018/)** — the rigorous intro.
- **[MIT 6.S898 / 9.S915 — Brain-Inspired Artificial Intelligence](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/MIT_Computer_Science_and_Artificial_Intelligence_Laboratory)** — DiCarlo, Boyden, Tenenbaum.
- **[Stanford CS 379C — Computational Models of the Neocortex](https://web.stanford.edu/class/cs379c/)** — graduate seminar.
- **[Princeton COS 495 / NEU 437 — Mathematical Tools for Neuroscience](https://compneuro.neuromatch.io/)** — open lecture notes.

## Foundational textbooks

- [Kandel et al. — Principles of Neural Science, 6e](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Principles_of_Neural_Science) — the canonical reference. Read selectively.
- [Dayan & Abbott — Theoretical Neuroscience](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Theoretical_neuroscience) — standard text.
- [Gerstner et al. — Neuronal Dynamics](https://neuronaldynamics.epfl.ch/) — free online, spiking emphasis.
- [Sterling & Laughlin — Principles of Neural Design](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Peter_Sterling_(neuroscientist)) — energy-efficiency-first view of brain design.
- [Sutton & Barto — RL 2nd ed.](http://incompleteideas.net/book/the-book-2nd.html) — free PDF.
- [Goodfellow, Bengio, Courville — Deep Learning](https://www.deeplearningbook.org/) — free.
- [Buzsáki — Rhythms of the Brain](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gy%C3%B6rgy_Buzs%C3%A1ki) — for dynamics.

## Software

- **[Brian2](https://briansimulator.org/)** — Python spiking simulator. Easiest entry.
- **[NEST](https://www.nest-simulator.org/)** — large-scale spiking, C++ kernel.
- **[NEURON](https://www.neuron.yale.edu/neuron/)** — biophysical detailed models.
- **[Nengo](https://www.nengo.ai/)** — Eliasmith's Neural Engineering Framework, builds Spaun-style cognitive models.
- **[snnTorch](https://snntorch.readthedocs.io/)** — surrogate-gradient SNNs in PyTorch.
- **[NetPyNE](http://www.netpyne.org/)** — high-level network construction over NEURON.
- **[CARLsim](http://www.socsci.uci.edu/~jkrichma/CARLsim/)** — GPU SNN simulator.
- **[BindsNET](https://github.com/BindsNET/bindsnet)** — PyTorch-based SNN library.

## Datasets and benchmarks

### Neural data
- **[Allen Brain Observatory](https://observatory.brain-map.org/visualcoding/)** — large-scale mouse visual cortex two-photon recordings.
- **[International Brain Lab](https://www.internationalbrainlab.com/)** — standardized ephys + behavior data across labs.
- **[DANDI Archive](https://dandiarchive.org/)** — community archive of neural data in NWB format.
- **[OpenNeuro](https://openneuro.org/)** — standardized neuroimaging.
- **[Steinmetz et al. Neuropixels](https://figshare.com/articles/dataset/Distributed_coding_of_choice_action_and_engagement_across_the_mouse_brain/9598406)** — large-scale mouse Neuropixels.

### Connectomics
- **[FlyEM Hemibrain](https://www.janelia.org/project-team/flyem/hemibrain)** — fly brain connectome.
- **[FlyWire](https://flywire.ai/)** — full female adult fly connectome.
- **[MICrONS](https://www.microns-explorer.org/)** — mouse cortical 1mm³ connectome.
- **[OpenWorm](http://openworm.org/)** — C. elegans full simulation.

### NeuroAI benchmarks
- **[Brain-Score](https://www.brain-score.org/)** — model-vs-cortex leaderboard for vision and increasingly other modalities.
- **[Algonauts](http://algonauts.csail.mit.edu/)** — visual cortex prediction challenge.
- **[Neural Latents Benchmark](https://neurallatents.github.io/)** — latent dynamics of motor cortex.

## Atlases & viewers

- **[Allen Brain Atlas](https://atlas.brain-map.org/)** — gene expression, anatomy, connectivity.
- **[BrainFacts.org](https://www.brainfacts.org/)** — public-facing accurate intro.
- **[Scholarpedia](http://www.scholarpedia.org/)** — high-quality refereed neuro encyclopedia.
